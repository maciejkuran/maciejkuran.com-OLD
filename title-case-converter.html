Title case converter

Title case is a style that is used for the titles of articles, books, movies, songs, and other works. It makes the title look elegant and readable.

All major words will be capitalized, while minor words (articles, coordinating conjunctions and other) will be lowercased: a, an, and, the, or, is, of, but, as, in

  • The man of the forest.
  • The Man of the Forest.
